Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the PrimeStart Program?
The PrimeStart Program includes complete trading education, step-by-step frameworks, daily live trading sessions, Discord community access, 24/7 mentorship support, trade analysis, and psychology coaching. You'll get everything needed to prepare for prop firm challenges.
Can I change my payment plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or modify your plan after enrollment. Contact our support team at primeverseofficial04@gmail.com or use the Discord support channel for plan adjustments.
How long is the program?
The PrimeStart Program is self-paced with lifetime access to materials. Most traders complete the core curriculum in 8-12 weeks, but you can progress at your own speed. Discord community and mentorship support remain available indefinitely.
Is there a refund policy?
PrimeVerse offers a non-refundable policy on digital programs. However, refunds are available only for duplicate charges or confirmed technical billing errors within 48 hours. Please refer to our Refund Policy for more details.
When will I get access?
After successful payment, you'll receive immediate access to all program materials. Our team will add you to the Discord community within 24 hours, and your mentorship will begin right away.
What's the difference between Partial Payment and Custom Program?
Partial Payment offers the standard PrimeStart curriculum in installments. Custom Program is tailored to advanced traders with personalized one-on-one mentorship and specialized strategies. Choose based on your current experience level.
